I’m best known for paying attention to details among my coworkers and friends.

After obtaining a Bachelor's degree in IT engineering, due to my exceptional grades, I was offered the opportunity to pursue a Master's degree in computer networking. However, after graduation and two years of working as a network engineer, I realized this field did not interest me. I then shifted my career to the banking industry, where I held various roles, such as evaluation engineer, product researcher, and project manager for seven years.

Alongside my professional career, I have always had the desire to help others, especially those who are underrepresented. This passion led me to volunteer with various charities. My love for assisting people and solving their problems, as well as my interest in the tech industry, eventually led me to UX design.

Upon arriving in the UK in early 2021, I pursued my dream. I began with an online UX course from Google and followed it up by enrolling in a program at the University of Birmingham. I’ve continued to volunteer with Cancer Research and CodeYourFuture. Within two months of volunteering, I was given the opportunity to become a Project Manager and UX Designer for one of their clients.

I am seeking a new role in the web, application, or product design field to utilize my design and research skills, enhance the user experience, and simplify complex processes.
